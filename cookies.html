What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or phone) when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and understand how you use the site.

How We Use Cookies

Cool Games uses cookies in the following ways:

• Google AdSense: We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google uses cookies to show you personalized ads based on your interests and browsing history. You can learn more about how Google uses cookies here.

• Website Functionality: Some cookies help our website work properly and remember your settings.

Types of Cookies We Use

• Advertising Cookies: Used by Google AdSense to deliver relevant advertisements
• Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly
• Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site (if any)

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in your browser settings. Here's how:

• Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
• Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
• Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies
• Edge: Settings → Privacy & Services → Cookies

You can also opt out of personalized advertising by visiting: Google Ads Settings

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our site, such as Google (for advertising). These services may use cookies to track your browsing across different websites.
